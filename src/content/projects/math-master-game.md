## Overview
Collaborated with a team to develop an engaging educational game designed to help students improve their math skills through gamified learning.

## Key Achievements
- **Team Collaboration:** Collaborated with a team of three, developing "Math Master" for students in grades 4-8 using Unity 2022 and Visual Studio 2019.
- **Version Control:** Managed codebase efficiently with GitHub for seamless team collaboration.
- **Game Design:** Designed a captivating educational experience with escalating difficulty and a unique vaporwave style.
- **Engagement:** The game challenges players to improve their math skills, driving engagement through competition for high scores.
