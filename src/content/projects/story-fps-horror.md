## Overview
Led and coordinated a story-driven first-person horror project that brought together developers, audio engineers, and artists under a single creative direction. The project combined team leadership with hands-on Unreal Engine blueprint development, requiring both technical execution and project-level coordination.

What made the work meaningful was the dual responsibility: keeping multiple contributors aligned while still building a large portion of the gameplay systems directly.

---

## Team & Production Structure

The project involved collaboration across several creative and technical groups:

- Game developers
- Audio engineers
- Artists

In addition to direction and coordination, I contributed directly to implementation so that production planning stayed grounded in what was technically achievable.

---

## Technical Implementation

Built the project in **Unreal Engine 4**, using blueprint-heavy development to create a wide range of gameplay systems.

- Developed **40+ blueprint programs**
- Implemented complex gameplay mechanics through actors, data structures, mesh alignment, materials, and animation behavior
- Used rapid visual scripting workflows to keep production moving while supporting a broader project vision

The project helped strengthen both technical problem-solving and leadership judgment because design ambitions constantly had to be balanced against time, team capacity, and gameplay clarity.

## Key Achievements
- **Direction:** Served in a leadership role coordinating developers, audio engineers, and artists around a shared horror-game vision.
- **Blueprint Development:** Built over 40 Unreal Engine blueprint systems to support gameplay progression and moment-to-moment mechanics.
- **Systems Design:** Produced complex in-game behavior through actors, data structures, mesh alignment, materials, animation, and related gameplay logic.
- **Cross-Team Coordination:** Kept technical work and creative direction aligned across multiple contributors and disciplines.
