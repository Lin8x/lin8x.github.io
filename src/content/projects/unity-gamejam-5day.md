## Overview
Worked within a team to create a functional video game in the span of 5 days, contributing both development and creative skills.

## Key Achievements
- **Audio Production:** Utilized Audacity to produce a total of 7 audio tracks for game music, sound effects, and in-game announcements.
- **UI Design:** Used Photoshop to design work UI which portrayed player health, enemy wave, ability usage, and other gameplay visuals.
- **Project Management:** Planned out tasks with team members to ensure that production of the game was efficient.
- **Multidisciplinary:** Served dual roles as front-end developer and audio designer.
