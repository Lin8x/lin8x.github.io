## Overview
Worked within a team to create a complete playable game in just five days, contributing both front-end development and audio design. The short timeline made the project a strong exercise in prioritization, scope control, and shipping usable work quickly instead of overdesigning features that could not be completed on time.

Because I contributed across both technical and creative areas, the project also helped build versatility under pressure. It required switching between implementation, presentation, and production needs without losing momentum.

---

## Project Constraints

The project was built inside a highly compressed game jam timeline:

- **Duration:** 5 days
- **Roles:** Front-end developer and audio designer
- **Focus:** Ship a functional and cohesive playable experience, not just isolated features

This meant every decision had to be evaluated through the lens of time, clarity, and whether it meaningfully improved the final build.

---

## Contributions

Supported both presentation and experience design:

- Built or supported the game’s user-facing front end
- Created **7 audio tracks** for music, sound effects, and in-game announcements
- Designed UI elements covering player health, enemy waves, abilities, and other gameplay feedback
- Worked with teammates to prioritize tasks and keep the project moving efficiently

The project was a good example of how smaller team efforts often require people to contribute outside of a single narrow specialty.

## Key Achievements
- **Multidisciplinary Contribution:** Served dual roles as front-end developer and audio designer in a rapid team-based production cycle.
- **Audio Production:** Produced 7 audio assets using Audacity for music, effects, and gameplay announcements.
- **UI Design:** Designed interface elements communicating player health, enemy waves, ability usage, and other game-state information.
- **Team Coordination:** Planned and prioritized tasks with teammates to keep production efficient inside a five-day deadline.
- **Jam Delivery:** Helped ship a functional game under extreme time constraints by balancing implementation and creative responsibilities.
